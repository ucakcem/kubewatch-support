Privacy & Data

Your cluster connection details (API URL, tokens, certificates) are stored only on your device in the iOS Keychain. We do not collect, transmit, or store your cluster data or personal information on our servers. KubeWatch is free on the App Store; we do not charge you for downloading or using the app.
