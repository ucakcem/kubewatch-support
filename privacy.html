KubeWatch

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

KubeWatch ("the app") is a Kubernetes cluster monitoring app for iOS. As of version 2.0, it is offered free of charge on the App Store. It lets you view pods, nodes, deployments, events, and logs from your clusters. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data or cluster data on our servers. Connection details and data you see in the app stay on your device or are sent only to the Kubernetes API servers you configure.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores cluster connection information (API server URL, bearer token, client certificates, CA certificate) in the iOS Keychain. Other preferences—such as refresh interval, notification settings, and which cluster is active—are stored locally on your device. None of this data is sent to us or to any third party for our own use. Demo mode uses only simulated data and does not involve real cluster credentials.

Cluster Data

When you connect to a Kubernetes cluster, the app requests data (pods, nodes, events, logs, etc.) directly from your cluster's API server. That data is displayed on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We do not log, store, or analyze your cluster data.

Kubernetes Access and Your Responsibility

You are responsible for ensuring that you have proper authorization to connect to any cluster you add in KubeWatch. Use of the app must comply with your organization's policies, applicable laws, and any contractual or regulatory requirements (e.g. data protection, access control, audit). We do not access your clusters and are not responsible for how you or your organization use the app. Kubernetes is a trademark of the Linux Foundation.

Pricing & App Store

KubeWatch is available free of charge on the App Store (version 2.0 onward). We do not charge you for downloading or using the app, and we do not handle or store payment information for app access. Your relationship with Apple for App Store use (including account and device settings) is governed by Apple's terms and privacy policy.

Analytics & Advertising

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising in the app. We do not track your usage or cluster activity for marketing or other purposes.
